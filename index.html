FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest scoreline in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers - and indeed in the history of international football - was recorded on 11 April 2001, when America beat America Samoa 31-0.

This legendary match also brought global praise to Archie Thompson, whose 13 goal haul set a new world record, which remains to this day, for an individual player in a major international match.

And though the defeat caused America Samoa questions, an inspiring tale of their subsequent recovery from that is now the subject of an acclaimed documentary, 'Next Goal Wins', showing across the world.
